Augustinians do not restrict their daily interactions with their own colleagues but to a broader dimension of events, ideas, and people."
Marie Ortaliz, Ed.D.
" CSA-B has changed me, molded me in a way I could never foreseen. This is when I use the experiences I have gained in this school and in life and I begin to demonstrate in more definite and long lasting ways: my determination, my mettle, my patience, my compassion, and my desire to contribute in this world through my chosen profession always in Augustinian way."
Engr. Jensen G. Moncera
"CSA-B is not only known to produce academically-driven graduates but also well-rounded professionals due to its training in leadership, arts, literature, sports and public relations. Oneness in every situation will always be my best memory of CSA-B."
Charisse Gangoso-Lu, CPA
